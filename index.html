Services

Mircroshading

Microshading is often used to enhance or reshape the eyebrows by depositing small dots of pigment into the skin, creating a soft, powdered effect. This technique is different from traditional eyebrow tattooing, as it aims to mimic the appearance of eyebrow powder or pencil rather than individual hair strokes. The result is a fuller and more defined eyebrow look.

Eyeliner

Permanent makeup eyeliner, also known as cosmetic eyeliner tattooing or micropigmentation for the eyes, is a procedure in which pigments are tattooed onto the skin to create the appearance of eyeliner. This technique is designed to enhance the eyes, define the lash line, and eliminate the need for daily eyeliner application.

Liptint

Permanent makeup liptint, also known as lip tattooing or lip micropigmentation, is a cosmetic procedure in which pigments are implanted into the lips to create the appearance of lip color. This technique aims to enhance the natural lip color, define the lip shape, and provide a long-lasting solution for individuals who want to add color and definition to their lips without the need for daily lipstick application. Some people choose this procedure to address issues such as pale or uneven lip color, the desire for fuller-looking lips, or the convenience of not having to apply lipstick regularly.
